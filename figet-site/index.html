About Me

I am a passionate designer and 3D printing enthusiast who loves exploring the possibilities of form, function, and fabrication. Each piece you see on this page reflects my journey through creativity, experimentation, and learning in the world of digital design and additive manufacturing. I started this business to help all of my friends at school who would not pay attention in class because they were always in search of the perfect fidget. I think that today I have at least made one. Thank you for your time and I hope you love these fidgets with many more coming soon.
